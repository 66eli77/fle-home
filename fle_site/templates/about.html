Our Mission

The Foundation for Learning Equality (FLE) is a startup non-profit organization located in San Diego, CA. We are dedicated to creating tools for the sharing and creation of open-licensed educational content for use by anyone around the world, with a special emphasis on reaching the 65% of the world that does not have Internet access, and those with limited-bandwidth, expensive, or unreliable connections.
