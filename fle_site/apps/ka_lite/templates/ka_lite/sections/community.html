Development

KA Lite is an open source project, and interested developers are encouraged to get involved. We welcome those at any proficiency level, from novice to expert, as long as you have a passion for providing free universal education and a desire to learn. Check out our Github Wiki to get started.

Testing

It is crucial to test various features of KA Lite on many different platforms in order to ensure that everything is running smoothly. If you would like to get involved by helping in this regard, you will first want to check out our Google groups page and also download and set up KA Lite. Start testing!

Seeding content

One of the downloading options for KA Lite is BitTorrent, and we are always looking for individuals who would be willing to seed the torrent download.

Translations

KA Lite now supports internationalization, and we rely on a worldwide network of translators for support. Help us translate.

Social Media

Follow us on Facebook and Twitter to keep up with all the latest news and updates!

Donate

As a non-profit organization, to support our developers, hosting costs, and other overhead, we rely on donations from people like you. Thanks for your support!
