Pre-installed Android Tablet

Ready to use out of the box
BARRIER

Downloading and installing Kolibri is an immediate barrier in low-bandwidth contexts. Tablets often depend on nearby servers for an internet connection.

SOLUTION

No hassling with internet connection or installation; Kolibri will run as a standalone Android application. We're working with manufacturers for Kolibri to be pre-installed on custom-made, low-cost tablets.

Learn More

Customized Classes

Curate unique sets of educational content
BARRIER

There's a need for educational material to fit the culture and context of any given learning community. Education is not "one-size-fits-all."

SOLUTION

Kolibri will allow organizations and users to upload and organize their unique educational content into customized "channels," with the ability to integrate existing channels such as Khan Academy. These channels can then be distributed to devices that run Kolibri for offline use.

Learn More

Thousands of Videos on One Memory Card

Fit entire content libraries on any device
BARRIER

Downloading educational content is slow and often impossible in areas with low or no internet connectivity. The amount of content that can be stored is often limited as well.

SOLUTION

We're developing an innovative offline tool to compress videos into a very low-bandwidth HTML5 format while retaining quality, so that thousands of videos can fit on a single 4GB device.

Try it out!

Offline Wireless Content Sharing

Sync and share between any devices
BARRIER

We already provide ways to leverage unreliable or nonexistant internet access, but syncing and sharing is still a challenge when devices are running in totally offline contexts.

SOLUTION

No internet? No problem. Devices running Kolibri will be able to connect to each other over local wifi or LAN connections to share content, update software, and sync data.
