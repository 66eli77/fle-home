Preconfigured Tablet

An affordable Android tablet that can run Kolibri smoothly

Why Android?

Android tablets are the most cost- effective solution for Kolibri’s needs. Kolibri requires hardware that can run a full fledged software platform with the ability to playback audio and video, sync with a central server, and have battery operated for times when the electric grid is unreliable.

We’ve had plenty of experience deploying Ka-Lite, our previous offline learning platform, using Raspberry Pi. Pi is an excellent deployment tool when run as a server, but is not suitable as a standalone learning machine. When it comes to the best hardware solution for Kolibri, an Android tablet is the clear winner.

Our target tablet will have:

4 Core Processor
1G RAM
8G Storage
7" IPS screen
GPS

Later this year, we will work with manufacturers to customize the tablet to ensure Kolibri runs efficiently on it, and using its full potential to achieve a cost- effective goal.

Content Curation

We're working on prototypes to build the tools we need to work with organizations that produce high quality content to create custom channels to distribute their content, and regional organizations and governments to customize and align this content to local curricular standards to serve the needs of local learners.

★ Remix existing channels: Kolibri will allow educational content producers and organizations to have the freedom to combine content from various channels to make a broad library of high quality and locally relevant educational content available to offline learners. For example, an instructor could create a new channel by combining math content from the Khan Academy channel and geography content from the OpenStax channel, allowing them to easily integrate a variety of learning material to customize their curriculum.

★ Upload new content: There are times when these content producers have come up with their own learning material that they have found works effectively. When this happens, they can upload their content to Kolibri, and then choose to make their new channel available to others who may find it useful for their own contexts.

★ Integrate new and existing content: Kolibri will allow educational content producers and organizations to combine existing content with their own. If an organization like UNICEF knows from experience that certain math lessons are favored by students, and they have also noticed that some of their own original exercises are effective, they can create a new channel and combine their content with pre-existing content from another channel to optimize their curriculum.

"Byte-Size" Videos

We're using an innovative video compressor which converts the video elements you see into scalable vector objects.

This is tech-talk for the idea that hundreds of gigabytes of videos can now be compressed into just a few megabytes. For people who only have access to lower-performing technology, this means they can download huge libraries of videos at a fraction of the time and space.

☝ Since this method allows videos to be composed of vectorized elements, you can select them on the video by clicking and dragging around them. You can change their position and size.
Try it while the video is playing!





Self-Paced and Customized Learning

Being able to measure one's learning progress is critical to taking the next step in educational growth. In contexts where getting a traditional formal education is not an option, tracking learning progress is not a simple matter. Thus, it is important for learners to be able to measure their progress at their own pace.

Kolibri will be installed with evaluation tools such as "coach reports," customized quizzes and exercises, and a recommendation tool that displays useful and relevant content that is based on how a learner engages with their content.

★ Coach Reports: These are detailed progress reports that measure factors such as time taken to complete exercises, records on which questions have been answered correctly or incorrectly, and provide a picture on overall progress through a specific set of exercises and lessons.

★ Customized Quizzes and Exercises: Question sets and exercises can be created in Kolibri in multiple choice and free response format. This customized evaluation tool will help learners practice what they've learned and show what they know, while staying aligned with the goals and standards of their local curriculum.
