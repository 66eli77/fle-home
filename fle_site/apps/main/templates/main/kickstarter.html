Content Curation

A tool for sharing your knowledge with people in need

★ Remix existing channels: Teachers and organizations have the freedom to combine content from various channels to create unique curriculum that caters to students’ specific needs. For example, an instructor could create a new channel by combining math content from the Khan Academy channel and geography content from the OpenStax channel, allowing them to easily integrate a variety of learning material to customize their curriculum.

★ Upload exclusively new content: There might be times when instructors have come up with their own learning material that they have found works effectively. When this happens, they can upload their content to Kolibri, in order to share it with other instructors who may find it useful for their own students.

★ Alternatively, one can rearrange an existing channel: Instructors also have the ability to customize content that already exists in a channel. If they think that certain lessons in a channel may be too challenging for their students, for example, they can remove these lessons and present their students only with the material that is appropriate for them.

★ Integrate new and existing content: Kolibri also allows instructors to combine existing content with their own. If an organization like UNICEF knows from experience that certain math lessons are favored by students, and they have also noticed that some of their own original exercises are effective, they can create a new channel and combine their content with pre-existing content from another channel to optimize their curriculum.
