How to Get to FLE

The FLE team offices out of Richard C. Atkinson Hall on the UCSD Campus.

The most convenient to place to park would be lot the parking lot across from Richard C. Atkinson Hall on UCSD's campus. It's $1 per 45 minutes for parking. If that lot is full, you will likely be able to find space in Hopkins Parking Structure which is just up the hill.

When you arrive, please contact the FLE team member that you are scheduled to meet with to receive further directions.
