How to Give

You can give by mail by making checks payable to "Foundation for Learning Equality" and sending them, along with your name and email address, to:

Foundation for Learning Equality
Attn: Jamie Alexandre
4465 Vision Dr. #2
San Diego, CA 92121

Alternatively, you can give online by submitting donations through our PayPal giving portal:

Donate to FLE through Paypal

The Foundation for Learning Equality Inc is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, so donations can be treated as tax-deductible contributions. Be sure to include your mailing address if you wish to receive a paper copy of your tax receipt, otherwise we will email you a PDF.

Why Give to FLE?

The Foundation for Learning Equality (FLE) actively develops and maintains open-source software that helps thousands of learners all over the world access the same high-quality educational materials available on the Internet that their peers in the developed world take for granted.

By making a contribution to FLE, you'll be supporting the further development of KA Lite, and future tools for the sharing and creation of open-licensed educational content for use by anyone around the world.
