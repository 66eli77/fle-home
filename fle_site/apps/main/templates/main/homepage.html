THE DIVIDE

Understand why universal education is our cause.
Poverty rates have greatly decreased over the past few decades. However, more than 1.5 billion people around the world are still afflicted by poverty.
WE BELIEVE Education is both the key to breaking the cycle of poverty and opening the door to human flourishing.
357 MILLION children are out of school. Half of them never had the chance to start their education.
_
+
250 MILLION children are in school but are not learning because of poor teacher training and infrastructure, and lack of learning materials.
That's
607 MILLION,
OR 1 IN 3
children in the world who lack access to a quality basic education.
There are now more resources online than ever before for accessing high quality educational content for free in any subject.
However,
4.3 BILLION
 ARE DISCONNECTED
This is the divide. The 60% of the world who could benefit the most from this tremendous opportunity lack the connectivity needed to access it.

THE BRIDGE

"Education is the most powerful weapon we can use to change the world."
- Nelson Mandela
A universal education means that anyone, online or offline, can access it. We bridge the divide by creating educational tools that are

Low Cost

Powerful, yet lightweight and inexpensive, with the ability to run on a wide variety of low-power and old hardware.

Made for Collaboration

Gives students and teachers the agency to collaborate together in the classroom rather than the traditional lecture.

Designed for Everyone

Adaptable to different learning needs, preferences, and cultures, empowering people to optimize and personalize their learning experience.
